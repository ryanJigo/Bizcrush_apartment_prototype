**What income-restricted means:** Some or all units at this community are offered under **affordable-housing rules**. Your **whole household’s** income (everyone who will live there, before taxes) must be **at or below a maximum limit** set for that unit type. **If your household earns more than that limit, you cannot lease that unit** — the program is specifically for people who do **not** have too much income for the tier they apply for. Limits depend on how many people are in the household and which unit you want; the property or listing will show the exact dollar amounts and how pay and assets are counted.

**Move-in special:** Up to 3 weeks free on select units — tour and apply within 24 hours to lock in (per listing).
